Dashboard sections

Hero stats — 3-column grid: Garments, Outfits, Wears. Big numbers, small uppercase labels.
Wear frequency bar chart — Accent-colored gradient bars by day of week. Visual at a glance.
Color palette — Horizontal stacked bar showing wardrobe color distribution with legend dots.
Category donut + Cost per wear — Side by side. Donut shows garment distribution, progress bars show value analysis.
Wardrobe health radar — 6-axis radar chart: Variety, Color, Usage, Season, Value, Fit. Shows balance at a glance.
All charts use accent color — Adapts to user's chosen accent. No hardcoded gold — these mockups show gold as example only.
