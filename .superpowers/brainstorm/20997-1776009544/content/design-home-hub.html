Design principles

Editorial section headers — Uppercase, wide letter-spacing, very low opacity. Vogue magazine section dividers.
Playfair Display greeting — The hero text uses the brand's editorial font. Everything else is system font / DM Sans for readability.
Minimal cards — Hairline borders, near-invisible backgrounds. Content speaks, not chrome.
Grouped by intent — "Your Stylist" (AI features) and "Discover" (exploration). Users know where to look.
